📖 EVENT OVERVIEW

Welcome to the most terrifying Minecraft event of the year! Navigate through a zombie-infested world, solve cryptic hints, collect ancient beacons, and survive the Warden's deadly maze. Only the bravest will claim the ultimate prize - the Nether Star of victory!

🎯 GAME PHASES

⚡ PHASE 1: BEACON HUNT

Objective: Find 5 hidden beacons scattered across the map

The Challenge:
• Follow cryptic hints to locate hidden chests containing beacons
• Race against 50+ other players to claim your beacon
• ZOMBIE APOCALYPSE: Endless zombies spawn from hidden spawners under mossy carpets
• Bring your beacon to spawn and place it to qualify for Phase 2

Environment:
• Zombie spawners hidden throughout the map under the first ground layer
• Covered with mossy carpet for camouflage
• Continuous zombie spawning creates constant danger

🌟 PHASE 2: WARDEN'S MAZE

Objective: Navigate the deadly maze to claim the Nether Star

The Final Challenge:
• Only the 5 beacon collectors advance to this phase
• Survive the labyrinthine maze filled with deadly Wardens
• Choose your strategy: Fight the monsters or sneak past them
• Reach the maze's end to claim the ultimate prize

Victory Condition:
• First player to reach the chest at the maze's end wins the Nether Star
• Becomes the Halloween Champion!

💡 ADDITIONAL SUGGESTIONS

🔊 Atmospheric Audio: Add custom horror sound effects through the resource pack - creepy ambient sounds, distant screams, and ominous music to amplify the fear factor.
📱 Communication System: Set up a Discord channel with proximity chat or walkie-talkie plugin for realistic team communication and added immersion.
💀 Death Consequences: Consider temporary spectator mode for eliminated players, allowing them to haunt and provide hints to remaining survivors.
🎭 Role Assignments: Give some players secret roles like "Infected" (can respawn as zombies) or "Medic" (can revive fallen players once).
⏰ Time Pressure: Add countdown timers for each phase to increase urgency and prevent camping strategies.
🏃 Movement Modifiers: Use potions to simulate fear - occasional slowness, nausea, or blindness effects when near Wardens.
📊 Spectator Entertainment: Create elevated viewing areas for eliminated players with good sightlines of the action.
🎁 Consolation Prizes: Offer smaller rewards for participation, creative deaths, or best teamwork to keep everyone engaged.
